Data Analysis Project

Supermarket Sales Dashboard

A comprehensive Excel dashboard analyzing 4 years of sales data across three major branches to identify growth trends and operational bottlenecks.

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables Data Cleansing

Key Metrics

  • Total Revenue: $322,966
  • Transactions: 1,000
  • Avg Rating: 6.97/10

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance for a supermarket chain with branches in Yangon, Mandalay, and Naypyitaw. The goal was to transform raw transaction data into an interactive dashboard that allows stakeholders to monitor financial health and customer behavior from 2019 to 2022.

By utilizing advanced Excel features, including Pivot Tables and Slicers, I created a tool that filters data by branch, product category, and time period, enabling data-driven decision-making.

Key Insights & Findings

1

Sales Trends & Seasonality

The data reveals a consistent growth trajectory peaking in 2021 with over $114k in revenue. However, a sharp decline was observed in Q1 2022, highlighting the need for immediate marketing intervention.

2

Operational Peak Hours

Footfall analysis indicates that the busiest operational hours are between 1:00 PM and 7:00 PM. Optimizing staff shifts during these windows could significantly improve service quality and customer ratings.

3

Customer Preferences

Payment methods are evenly split between E-wallets, Cash, and Credit Cards (~33% each), suggesting a digitally mature customer base. Product-wise, the Naypyitaw branch leads in "Food & Beverages" sales, while Yangon excels in "Home & Lifestyle".

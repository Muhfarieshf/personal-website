Retail Profit Analysis with SAS

Advanced statistical modeling (ANOVA & Regression) to identify key revenue drivers across product categories using SAS 7.1.

Read Report Download PDF

Methodology

Utilized SAS 7.1 to perform Linear Regression and Analysis of Variance (ANOVA). The goal was to test hypotheses regarding the relationship between product categories (e.g., Golf, Running) and profit margins.

Key Findings

The analysis revealed significant profit variances (Prob > F < .0001) in categories like Children's Sports and Outdoor Gear, helping to pinpoint high-margin inventory for strategic stocking.

Impact

Delivered a data-backed proposal for inventory optimization. The findings informed membership decisions and highlighted underperforming categories requiring promotional intervention.

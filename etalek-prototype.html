Etalék: Empowering Youth Coding

A mobile application designed to simplify coding education for Malaysian youth through gamification and interactive learning modules.

View Prototype

The Design Process

Phase 1 & 2

Empathize & Define

We began by understanding the challenges Malaysian students face in learning coding: lack of engagement, complex jargon, and limited accessibility.

Problem Statement

"Students perceive coding as difficult and boring. Existing tools lack localized context and fail to sustain long-term motivation."

Phase 3

Ideation & Wireframing

We brainstormed features focusing on gamification (badges, leaderboards) and bite-sized lessons. The "Etalék" concept was born—a digital storefront for knowledge.

  • Interactive Quizzes
  • Progress Tracking Dashboard
  • Community Forum
Phase 4

User Flow & Architecture

Before moving to high-fidelity designs, we mapped out the user's journey to ensure a logical and intuitive navigation experience from login to lesson completion.

Phase 4 & 5

High-Fidelity Prototype

The final design was built in Figma, featuring a clean, vibrant UI tailored for youth engagement. Below is the interactive prototype used for user testing.

App Features

Gamified Learning

Points, badges, and leaderboards to motivate consistent practice.

Curated Modules

Structured lessons ranging from "Hello World" to advanced algorithms.

Peer Support

A community forum for students to ask questions and share projects.
